The Problem

New Zealand's industrial ecosystem is deeply fragmented

Millions in high-end equipment sit idle. Startups struggle to access what they need. And an entire generation of expertise is walking out the door.

"I was in a kitchen with a small business owner who was in tears — incredibly frustrated because a processing kettle she had just spent her limited cash on wasn't working as she'd hoped. The toughest part was knowing that a larger, perfectly capable kettle was just sitting unused in storage at UoA."

— Real conversation, the reason equipA.kiwi exists
🏚️

Stranded Capital

Millions in high-end R&D equipment — mass spectrometers, bioreactors, flow cytometers — sit idle at CRIs and universities outside grant windows, earning nothing.

📦

Complex Procurement

Food businesses and manufacturers navigate a labyrinth of dealers, auctioneers, and hire companies with no unified portal, no price transparency, and no trusted network.

🧠

The Knowledge Cliff

A looming 25% workforce retirement will permanently erase decades of operational expertise on complex industrial machinery — unless we act now to capture it.

🌍

Export Barriers

Scaling a food product from bench-top to commercial export volumes requires prohibitive capital investment that locks out New Zealand's most innovative SMEs.

The Platform

A full-stack ecosystem, not just a classifieds site

Eight interconnected modules — each solving a distinct layer of the industrial disconnect — that together form Aotearoa's circular equipment economy.

Marketplace

Market

New, used, and refurbished machinery in one place. From CNC mills to centrifuges — transparent pricing, verified listings, and direct connections to NZ's leading equipment dealers and hire fleets.

Streamlines complex industrial procurement from months to days
Shared Access

Lab

A shared access portal connecting businesses to underutilised R&D and testing equipment at universities, CRIs, CoREs, and innovation parks. Book time on a mass spectrometer like a meeting room.

Unlocks idle assets at CRIs and universities for commercial R&D
Maintenance

Care

A lifecycle management and repair network connecting SMEs directly with certified local service technicians, calibration specialists, and heavy riggers for any piece of industrial equipment.

Connects every asset purchase to the expert who can keep it running
Compliance

Safe

A compliance hub integrating custom SOPs, safety checklists, and operational documentation. Aligned with WorkSafe NZ guidelines and LEENZ lifting regulations — built into every equipment listing.

Streamlines H&S compliance for every machine in the ecosystem
Knowledge

Learn

An education and industrial training hub capturing legacy knowledge from retiring operators and transferring it to apprentices. Video libraries, machine-specific guides, and expert operator networks.

Preserves and transfers decades of specialist expertise before it retires
Circularity

Loop

A circular economy portal facilitating trade-ins, refurbishment pathways, parts salvaging, and carbon tracking. Turns decommissioned assets into productive resources instead of landfill.

Closes the loop between end-of-life assets and new productive use
Finance

Finance

Flexible financing options making capital-heavy equipment accessible to SMEs. Lease-to-own, fractional ownership models, and structured payment plans designed for NZ's industrial sector.

Lowers the capital barrier to productive infrastructure ownership
Insurance

Sure

An integrated insurance and risk-management portal covering transit, mechanical breakdown, and liability for shared-access and high-value industrial assets. De-risks every transaction in the ecosystem so owners and operators can engage with confidence.

Removes the final barrier to sharing, moving, and financing high-value plant
